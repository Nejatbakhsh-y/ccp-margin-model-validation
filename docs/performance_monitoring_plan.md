## Validation Governance and Evidence

Ongoing monitoring is governed by Section 13 of
`docs/validation_charter.md`.

Approved monitoring requirements must be documented using:

`docs/templates/ongoing_monitoring_requirements_template.md`

Monitoring metrics and breaches must be recorded in:

- `reports/evidence/monitoring/monitoring_register.csv`
- `reports/evidence/monitoring/monitoring_breach_register.csv`

Monitoring breaches must be investigated, preserved, escalated, and assessed
for formal finding issuance or revalidation in accordance with the validation
charter.
